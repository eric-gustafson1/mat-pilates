About Pilates in the Ranch

person

Molly

Molly is a long-time Highlands Ranch resident, wife, and mother of two. She has become a devoted fitness advocate and teacher, specializing in functional exercise and pain-free movement.

people

Friends

Molly believes your workout should help you function better in your daily life, never injure you, and leave you feeling better than when you started.

spa

Studio Space

In her own studio, Molly integrates her Pilates, group fitness, and Arthritis Foundation training to create customized exercise programs for her clients. Part teacher, part cheerleader, Molly helps clients define and achieve their personal fitness goals.

verified_user

Credentials

Molly did her initial training and certification through Body Arts and Sciences International (BASI) and began teaching at Pilates Denver in 2014. Molly obtained additional certification through the Pilates Method Alliance (PMA), becoming a PMA-CPT in 2015. In her six years with Pilates Denver, Molly also taught group fitness classes including barre, but her passion remains Pilates.
